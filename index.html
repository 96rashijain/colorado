🏔️Altitude Sickness Prevention & Treatment

⚠️ Warning Signs

• Headache, nausea
• Dizziness, fatigue
• Shortness of breath
• Loss of appetite
• Difficulty sleeping

🛡️ Prevention Tips

• Ascend gradually
• Drink 3-4 liters water daily
• Avoid alcohol & caffeine
• Eat light, frequent meals
• Rest frequently

💊 Treatment Options

• Mild: Rest, hydrate, descend slightly
• Moderate: Acetazolamide (consult doctor)
• Severe: Immediate descent + medical help
• Emergency: Call 911
